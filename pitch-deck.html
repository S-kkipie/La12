01 The problem — with real money

Banks skip the barrio club.
Fans have no way in.

Small football clubs — second division, regional cups, neighborhood sides — are real businesses: gate takings, merch, fiercely loyal fans, room to grow.

But nobody lends them capital. The bank won't touch the neighborhood club; all that's left is expensive loan sharks.

On the other side: thousands of fans who want to back their club — and win alongside it.

There has never been an instrument to cross that gap. A huge void between the club and its people.

02 The solution

Tokenized revenue-share

A club raises capital in USD₮ and, in return, pays out a % of a visible income stream — the gate — for N months, up to a cap multiple.

The fan invests USD₮, receives an ERC-20 token = their stake, and claims their share of every payout — all in their self-custody wallet. No bank. No intermediary. The platform never custodies a cent.

The cold-start killer

The bank won't back the barrio club — the fan will. They invest for love and return. That kills the trust problem that sinks every new marketplace.

04 How it works — the demo

Two accounts, one loop

The fan

Invest & get paid

Sign up → an embedded WDK wallet creates itself (Yape-style, no seed in your face) → fund USD₮ → invest in a club's round → hold the ERC-20 share → claim payouts → withdraw. The keys are always yours.

The club

Raise & distribute

Sign up → collection wallet → publish a round (goal, %, cap, deadline). When the goal fills, the capital arrives. Push a distribution when the gate money lands.

The contract — RevenueShareRound — is an ERC-20 share with a claim-based dividend accumulator: gas-safe with any number of holders, 30/30 tests, adversarially hardened.

The wow moment

Club distributes revenue. Contract withholds the agreed % and splits it pro-rata to holders — automatically, on-chain.

USD₮ lands in the fan's wallet. No oracle. No trust. Everything on-chain, everything in USD₮.

05 Automatic by design new

Nobody flips a switch

Auto-close funding

Goal hit → capital sweeps

The moment a round reaches its goal (or its deadline passes), the raised USD₮ sweeps to the club on-chain — a permissionless call, sponsor-paid, no admin required. The database is corrected from the contract's real state, never trusted.

Club discovery

Browse clubs raising now

A homepage and a /clubs directory surface every club currently raising. Pick one, open the round, invest in a tap. The marketplace has a front door.

08 Risk #1 — we name it before the judge does

Revenue-share, not equity

Issuing "shares" means stepping into securities regulation. So we start with revenue-share / economic rights — a light, non-custodial instrument — plus self-custody: the user holds their keys, the platform holds no funds.

Precedent not to repeat

Football Index collapsed as opaque gambling with no real ownership. Us: on-chain ownership, transparent, self-custody.
